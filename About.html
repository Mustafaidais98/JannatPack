About Us

Jannat Pack is a leading manufacturer of high-quality, durable polystyrene (PS) foam food trays based in Hebron, Palestine. We proudly serve clients across the region, delivering innovative packaging solutions for the food industry.

With a strong commitment to sustainability, we specialize in producing eco-friendly PS foam trays that meet international safety and hygiene standards. Our trays are designed for strength, insulation, and affordability—perfect for meat, produce, bakery items, and ready-to-eat meals.

Our manufacturing facility uses state-of-the-art extrusion and thermoforming equipment to ensure product consistency and durability. Each tray undergoes rigorous quality control to ensure it meets customer expectations for appearance, function, and sustainability.

We believe in responsible production. Our trays are made using recyclable materials and production methods that reduce environmental impact. By choosing our products, clients not only receive outstanding packaging but also support eco-conscious manufacturing.
